More about us

This group comprises of past participants, Z Ambassadors, and people who are generally eager to learn pogramming and other related fields.

The annual IBM Z contest is one that seeks to broaden the skillset of individuals by introducing them to advanced systems and enable individuals gain practical knowledge. As every participant is given access to technologies such as IBM's super mainframe computers and LinuxOne system which server instances.

Participants also learn new languages and probably revise ones they already knew. Languages include Python, Rexx, Cobol, Bash.
